DRIVE

DRIVE is an interactive map that follows a car as it drives through the city of San Francisco. Each point on the map is interactive, and for each point you click on, it will take you to that part of the video. Each point also has a picture that relates to the area the car passes through.



Telecomn By Koby Kayden

Telecomn is a video that tracks the lies given out from phone carriers in the United States. From looking at all the different cell phone carriers in America, I noticed that they all are more or less very similar. From seeing all these similarities I wanted to put together a clip that proves how all of the major carriers advertise the same message over and over, whether it be in their: network speed, size, popularity, etc... This project is in NO way meant to glorify any of the companies shown. This project IS meant to show how similar the lies from these companies are.
